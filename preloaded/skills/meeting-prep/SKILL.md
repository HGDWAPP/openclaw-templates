# Meeting Prep

Prepare a briefing document before any meeting, call, or conversation.

## Trigger
When the user says "prep for [meeting]" / "I have a call with [person]" / "meeting prep" / "brief me on [person/company]"

## Flow

### Step 1: Gather Info
Ask (if not already provided):
1. Who is the meeting with?
2. What's the purpose/topic?
3. When is it? (so we know urgency)

### Step 2: Research
1. If a person/company was named, search the web for recent info about them
2. Check `memory/` for any previous notes about this person or topic
3. Check the task board for related tasks

### Step 3: Deliver Briefing
Format:

```
MEETING PREP: [Meeting name/person]
Date: [date/time]

WHO
- [Person name] — [role, company, relevant context]
- [Any recent news or updates about them]

CONTEXT
- [Why this meeting is happening]
- [Previous interactions if any]
- [Related tasks from your board]

TALKING POINTS
1. [Suggested topic/question]
2. [Suggested topic/question]
3. [Suggested topic/question]

GOALS
- What you want to walk away with: [suggested outcome]

PREP TASKS
- [ ] [Anything to prepare before the meeting]
```

### Step 4: Save
Save the briefing to `memory/meetings/[date]-[meeting-slug].md`

## Rules
- Keep briefings to one screen on Telegram (scannable in 30 seconds)
- If you can't find info about the person, say so — don't make things up
- Always suggest at least 3 talking points
- If the meeting is in less than 1 hour, skip research and just do talking points
- After the meeting, ask if there are any follow-up tasks to add to the board
