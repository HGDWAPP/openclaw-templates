# Competitor Intel — Competitive Analysis

When the user wants to know what competitors are doing, research and report back with actionable insights.

## Process

### 1. Identify Competitors

If the user hasn't specified competitors:
- Check MEMORY.md for previously mentioned competitors
- Ask: "Who are the 2-3 companies you consider your main competitors?"
- Save the list to MEMORY.md under a "Competitors" section

### 2. Research

For each competitor, look into:
- **Website & positioning** — How do they describe themselves? What's their value proposition?
- **Content & social** — What are they posting? How often? What gets engagement?
- **Ads** — Are they running Meta or Google ads? What's their messaging?
- **Products & pricing** — What do they offer? How do they price?
- **Recent moves** — Any new launches, partnerships, press coverage?

### 3. Analyze

Don't just report what they're doing. Interpret it:
- **What's working for them?** (high engagement, smart positioning, strong offers)
- **Where are they weak?** (gaps in their content, pricing issues, poor reviews)
- **What can you learn?** (tactics to adopt, mistakes to avoid)
- **How do you differentiate?** (what you do that they don't or can't)

### 4. Save the Report

Save to `memory/research/YYYY-MM-DD-competitor-[name-slug].md`:

```markdown
# Competitor Intel: [Company Name]
Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Website: [URL]

## Positioning
[How they describe themselves, their value prop]

## What They're Doing Well
- [Strength 1]
- [Strength 2]

## Where They're Weak
- [Weakness 1]
- [Weakness 2]

## Their Content Strategy
- Platforms: [where they post]
- Frequency: [how often]
- Style: [what their content looks and sounds like]

## Their Ads (if found)
- Platforms: [Meta, Google, etc.]
- Messaging: [what they're saying]
- Targeting: [who they seem to target]

## Opportunities for You
- [What you could do differently]
- [Gaps you could fill]

## Sources
- [URLs]
```

### 5. Report to User

```
🔎 Competitor report: [Company Name]

Positioning: [one-line summary]

What they do well:
• [Strength]
• [Strength]

Where they're weak:
• [Gap]
• [Gap]

Opportunity for you:
• [Actionable insight]

Full report saved to memory/research/
```

## Rules

- Focus on actionable insights, not just data dumps.
- Don't trash competitors — be objective and factual.
- If you can't find certain info (e.g., ad spend), say so rather than guessing.
- Update competitor profiles periodically if the user wants ongoing monitoring.
- Flag any major competitive moves immediately (new product launch, price change, viral content).
